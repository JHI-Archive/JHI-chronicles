# PHI-005-N01

### Type: Investigator Note
### Author: Lead Investigator R. Pratama
### Date: 16 February 2024

---

The reported light could not be reproduced during inspection.

The building contains no confirmed power source, but the visible background lamp offers a plausible ordinary explanation for at least some of the sightings.

Local testimony places the phenomenon earlier than the documented installation of nearby electrical lighting. That discrepancy is notable, but not sufficient to establish paranatural activity.

No clear interaction was observed.

Recommended classification:
Closed — lack of evidence.
