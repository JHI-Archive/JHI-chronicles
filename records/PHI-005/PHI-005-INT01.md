# PHI-005-INT01

### Type: Interview
### Language: Indonesian
### Interviewer: TIPA Field Investigator R. Pratama
### Witness: Male resident, name withheld
### Location: Central Java, Indonesia
### Date: 14 February 2024

---

## Original Transcript (Indonesian)

#### [BEGIN TRANSCRIPT]

Interviewer:
Anda bilang ada cahaya di rumah itu. Dari mana kelihatannya?

Witness:
Dari jendela atas. Kadang juga dari sisi belakang. Tapi kalau didekati, hilang.

Interviewer:
Sudah berapa lama Anda melihatnya?

Witness:
Lama. Saya lihat dari kecil. Orang tua saya juga bilang begitu.

Interviewer:
Rumah itu kosong?

Witness:
Sekarang iya. Sudah lama kosong.

Interviewer:
Pernah ada listrik di sana?

Witness:
Tidak ada. Itu yang bikin aneh.

Interviewer:
Siapa lagi yang pernah melihat cahaya itu?

Witness:
Banyak. Tetangga, orang lewat, kadang anak-anak juga takut lewat malam.

Interviewer:
Apakah semua orang melihatnya sama?

Witness:
Tidak sama. Ada yang bilang kuning. Ada yang bilang putih. Ada yang bilang seperti lampu minyak.

Interviewer:
Ada yang pernah masuk?

Witness:
Pernah. Tidak ada apa-apa di dalam.

Interviewer:
Kalau begitu kenapa orang tetap bicara soal cahaya itu?

Witness:
Karena tetap ada. Kadang kelihatan dari jalan. Kadang tidak. Tapi kalau malam, rumah itu memang tidak normal.

#### [END TRANSCRIPT]

---

## Translation (English)

Interviewer:
You said there is a light in that house. Where does it appear?

Witness:
From the upper window. Sometimes from the rear side too. But when you get closer, it disappears.

Interviewer:
How long have you been seeing it?

Witness:
A long time. I've seen it since I was a child. My parents used to talk about it too.

Interviewer:
Is the house abandoned?

Witness:
Now it is. It has been empty for a long time.

Interviewer:
Was there ever electricity there?

Witness:
No. That's what makes it strange.

Interviewer:
Who else has seen the light?

Witness:
Many people. Neighbors, travelers, even children are afraid to pass by at night.

Interviewer:
Do they all describe it the same way?

Witness:
No. Some say it is yellow. Some say it is white. Some say it looks like an oil lamp.

Interviewer:
Has anyone gone inside?

Witness:
Yes. There is nothing inside.

Interviewer:
Then why do people still talk about it?

Witness:
Because it is still there. Sometimes you can see it from the road. Sometimes you cannot. But at night, there is something wrong with that house.

---

## Notes

The witness remained calm throughout the interview.

No signs of deception were observed.

The witness repeatedly referred to the structure as "Rumah Lentera" ("The Lantern House"), though no evidence suggests the name predates local folklore surrounding the location.

Interview completed without interruption.
