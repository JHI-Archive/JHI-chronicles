## Translation

**Interviewer:**
Who came to you at night?

**Witness:**
The old woman.

**Interviewer:**
Where does she stand?

**Witness:**
By the window. She stops there and keeps asking me things.

**Interviewer:**
What does she say?

**Witness:**
She says, “Because you do not want to leave.”

**Interviewer:**
Does she enter the house?

**Witness:**
No. She only stands there and looks at me.

**Interviewer:**
Do you know her?

**Witness:**
No... but her eyes are very big. And she wears something old.

**Interviewer:**
How many times did you see her?

**Witness:**
Every night. If I sleep a little, she comes back.

**Interviewer:**
Who knows about this?

**Witness:**
My uncle says I am sick in my head.

**Interviewer:**
Did he see her?

**Witness:**
He says no. But he does not want to listen to me.

---

## Notes

The witness became visibly distressed during questioning and showed signs of sleep deprivation. Several pauses in the transcript were caused by crying and repeated requests to stop the interview.

The witness also described being afraid to return home.

Interview terminated early for welfare review.
