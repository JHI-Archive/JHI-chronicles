## Description

Photograph depicts the exterior wall beneath the witness's bedroom window.

Observed features include:

- Extensive weathering of the exterior plaster.
- Multiple vertical cracks extending from the window frame.
- Areas of irregular discoloration beneath the sill.
- No visible footprints, tool marks, or signs of forced access.

Investigators noted that the witness consistently identified this window as the location where the reported figure appeared.

No person was present at the time the photograph was taken.

---

## Investigator Remarks

No feature visible in the photograph can be conclusively linked to the reported interaction.

The image was retained due to the witness's repeated identification of the location and because several observed markings could not be matched to earlier municipal photographs of the property.

No further conclusions were reached.
