# PHI-005 — The Lantern House

### Category: Location Interaction
### Risk Level: Low
### Reliability Index: C
### Status: Closed
### Location: Central Java, Indonesia

---

## Summary

PHI-005 refers to recurring reports of a light observed inside an abandoned rural structure after sunset.

Residents of a nearby village stated that the building appeared illuminated from within despite having no documented electrical connection. Several witnesses claimed the light could be seen from the road, but appeared to shift or dim when approached directly. A number of accounts also described a persistent feeling of being watched while facing the structure.

TIPA personnel inspected the site on multiple occasions. No active light source was found inside the building during investigation. The structure was unoccupied, and no evidence of recent habitation was recovered. A photograph taken during the survey captured the exterior of the house at night, with a distant light source visible behind it.

Review of municipal records suggested the visible glow may have corresponded to a streetlamp located beyond the property. However, local testimony stated that observations of the light predated the installation of nearby electrical lighting by several years.

Due to insufficient evidence and the absence of a reproducible paranatural effect, the case was closed as unsubstantiated.

## Notes

The structure remained a point of unease within the local community after closure of the file.

One witness stated that the light “never looked the same from the road as it did from the field.”

No further monitoring was authorized.
